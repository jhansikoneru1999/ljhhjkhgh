Engagement hall

An engagement or betrothal is the period of time between a marriage proposal and the marriage itself . During this period, a couple is said to be fiancés (from the French), betrothed, intended, affianced, engaged to be married, or simply engaged. Future brides and grooms may be called fiancée or fiancé, the betrothed, a wife-to-be or husband-to-be, respectively.
